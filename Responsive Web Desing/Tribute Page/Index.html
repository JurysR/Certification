Mini Bio

Ryan Rodney Reynolds was born on October 23, 1976, in Vancouver, Canada. Ryan Rodney Reynolds is a Canadian-American actor, film producer, and entrepreneur. He established his persona as a charismatic, quirky and quick-witted smart aleck in a wide range of Canadian and Hollywood films. Mainly known for his comedic performances, particularly as the star of the superhero franchise 'Deadpool'.
